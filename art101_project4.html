Artist's Statement

For this project, I wanted to gamify gardening. The initial proposal was to make a card game where player raises up their "soldier card" before they can use them; however, the project was too large to be completed in the time limit, so I ended up making a click and react game. In this game, the player aims to collect water for their crop, while also fending off pest that would attack the crop.

I used these p5js libraries below: p5js scene manager, and p5js play.
