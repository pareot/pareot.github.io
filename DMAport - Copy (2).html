Bee

For the 1st project in Art 103, my group decided to do a laser cut wood puzzle about bee. This is my depiction of bee. The work was done in Illustrator.

Tessellation

Another piece done in Illustrator--this piece is my individual design of Tessellation. The inspiration was taken from a character named Wortox in the game Don't Starve Together--you can see his tail in the design.

Map

This is a photoshopped image done on the map of the game Don't Starve Together. I wanted to create an image of sort to use as background for my website. The image was taken when I tried to rush finding an island in the game called Lunar Island--the bright yellow spot is where the island is; I also added decorative piece to the four corners of the map.

Tessellation Big Wall

I designed this large tesselation piece after the first exercise in Art103 so as to use as my background.

Digital Wheel

This work was created using java and Processing, then produced into video through Premier and Encoder. The main theme of this piece came from the function random--a variety of rectangle shapes appear in the background in variety of gray adds depth to the piece. The wheel in front is created by many draw shape functions--the goal of this wheel is to add some stability to the work. The waves along the four borders were originally more wide and flowy, however while I still keep that version in the code, I decided this jerking waves fit the overall aesthetic of the piece more.
