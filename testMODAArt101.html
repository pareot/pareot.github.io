Digital Wheel

Digital Wheel

I craeted this work with inspiration from randomness. The first visual experience the viewers see would likely be the cog-like wheel, the color-shifting diamond shape in the center of the wheel and the wave along the border of the painting. However, those imagery are common in today's daily experience, what bring depth to the piece is the random appreance of various rectangle shapes in the background--build ontop of one another, they give off the feeling of being pushed into the background, thus creating a visual effect of the painting moving forward.
