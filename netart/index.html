Artistic statement (cause every artists have one.

I have always had a keen interest in movement and idea--in fact, if it does not move nor possess an interesting concept, I usually have low interest toward the piece. After reading the article of Mark Tribe, I came to a realize that I associate with New Media art's "collaboration and participation" nature--I want to create a work where, instead of having viewers passively watch the piece, the viewers' experience will change accordingly to their interaction with the piece, a good example would be one such as Olia Lialina's "My Boyfriend Came Back from the War", but of course more up to time in term of technology.
