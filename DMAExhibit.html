ShellOut

ShellOut aims to depict the interaction between the inner-child and its host. The inner-child is the manifestation of a person's all around needs. Today, we--the host--tend to overlook our needs in favor of our goal. We sacrifice health to work overtime and hazardous works. We forego friends and family to chase connections. We are uneasy when we have time for ourselves, then try to fill it with to-dos.
