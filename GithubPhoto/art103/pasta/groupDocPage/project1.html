CONTEXT: WHAT IS OUR PROJECT ABOUT?

The final phase of the project reveals 175 bee themed pieces that fasten together with small dovetail joints. Altogether, they create a linear puzzle that describes the way our world thrives with the help of bees. Without them, we wouldn't have an ecosystem that provides crops, which benefit the our economic systems and provides nourishment for all beings. We would also lack certain commerical products that feed our economy, including cosmetics, candles, and even medicine. The idea of exploring the ways bees provide for our economy and our ecosystem (and basically our way of life) sounded much more promising than taking up more discouraging stories about bees and pesticides. We wanted to keep the positivity and be able to show our viewers just how large of an effect bees have on our world. Hopefully this would garner more appreciation for the little critters.
