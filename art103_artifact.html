Artist's Statement

In this world, dinossaurs live along side human. Given what we have known about ourselves, the answer was clear that either us or dinosaurs would be compromised if both are to exist on this planet. Archeologists managed to find evidence of dinosaur domestication: An item that was identified as a dinosaur cap was found in a recent excavation. The cap wasn't fully preserved, fornunately, the majority of the cap was still recognizable. Analysis suggests that this dinosaur might have been used by this group of human as a transporatation: The size of the dinosaur is perfect to carry one adult, maybe even an extra weight of a toddler.
